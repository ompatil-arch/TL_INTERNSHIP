Session 2

Expert Session: Digital Visibility

Documentation on Expert Session: Digital Visibility

-Dhananjay Kumbhar sir

Introduction

An expert session on Digital Visibility was conducted by Mr. Dhananjay Kumbhar, Assistant Professor and Director of Training and Placement at Sanjivani University. The session focused on the importance of building a strong professional presence in the digital world and how students can use online platforms effectively to improve their career opportunities. Through various real-life examples and practical insights, the speaker explained how a well-maintained digital profile can help students connect with industry professionals, attract recruiters, and secure better job opportunities.

Understanding Digital Visibility

Digital visibility refers to how easily a person can be found, recognized, and evaluated on online platforms. In today's competitive job market, recruiters often review a candidate's online presence before offering interviews or employment opportunities.

A strong digital presence helps individuals:

  • Showcase their skills and achievements.

  • Build professional credibility.

  • Connect with industry experts.

  • Access internships and job opportunities.

  • Create a positive professional image.

The session emphasized that students should treat their digital profiles as their online resume and professional identity.

Importance of LinkedIn

One of the major topics discussed during the session was the importance of LinkedIn as a professional networking platform.

Benefits of LinkedIn

  • Connects students with professionals and recruiters.

  • Provides access to internship and job opportunities.

  • Allows users to showcase projects, certifications, and achievements.

  • Helps in building a professional network.

  • Enables direct interaction with industry experts.

The speaker encouraged students to regularly update their LinkedIn profiles with:

  • Educational qualifications

  • Technical skills

  • Certifications

  • Projects

  • Internships

  • Achievements and extracurricular activities

A complete and professional LinkedIn profile increases visibility among recruiters and hiring managers.

Building Meaningful Professional Connections

A key takeaway from the session was the importance of creating quality professional connections rather than simply increasing the number of connections.

Students were advised to connect with:

  • Industry professionals

  • Recruiters

  • HR managers

  • Alumni

  • Faculty members

  • Technical experts

  • Company employees working in their field of interest

The speaker emphasized that connecting with people who can provide career guidance, referrals, mentorship, or placement opportunities is more beneficial than making unnecessary connections without professional value.

Digital Reputation and Personal Branding

The session highlighted that every online activity contributes to a person's digital reputation.

Personal Branding Includes:

  • Professional profile photo

  • Clear and informative bio

  • Consistent sharing of achievements

  • Participation in professional discussions

  • Display of skills and expertise

A strong personal brand helps students stand out among thousands of candidates applying for similar positions.

Role of Digital Accounts in Career Growth

Digital accounts are no longer used only for social interaction; they have become important career development tools.

Benefits of Maintaining Professional Digital Accounts

  • Increased visibility to recruiters.

  • Faster access to job openings.

  • Networking opportunities.

  • Professional recognition.

  • Portfolio showcasing.

  • Career advancement opportunities.

Students were encouraged to regularly maintain and update their online profiles to ensure that their achievements and skills are visible to potential employers.

Reducing Lengthy Recruitment Processes

The speaker explained that a strong digital presence can significantly reduce the time spent in recruitment processes.

When recruiters can easily view:

  • Skills

  • Certifications

  • Projects

  • Recommendations

  • Professional achievements

they gain confidence in a candidate's abilities even before the interview stage.

As a result:

  • Candidates may receive direct interview calls.

  • Recruiters can evaluate profiles more quickly.

  • Opportunities for referrals increase.

  • Hiring decisions become faster.

This demonstrates how digital visibility can help students gain a competitive advantage in the job market.

Real-Life Examples and Practical Insights

The session included several daily-life examples to demonstrate how digital visibility impacts professional growth. The speaker showed how individuals with strong online profiles often receive better networking opportunities, career guidance, and job referrals compared to those who remain inactive on professional platforms.

The examples helped students understand that maintaining a professional digital identity is now an essential part of career development rather than an optional activity.

Best Practices for Students

To improve digital visibility, students should:

  1. Create and maintain a professional LinkedIn profile.

  2. Use a professional profile photograph.

  3. Update achievements regularly.

  4. Showcase projects and certifications.

  5. Connect with industry professionals and alumni.

  6. Participate in professional discussions and communities.

  7. Follow companies related to their career interests.

  8. Build a positive digital reputation.

  9. Avoid sharing inappropriate or unprofessional content.

  10. Continuously learn and display new skills online.

Conclusion

The Digital Visibility session by Mr. Dhananjay Kumbhar provided valuable insights into the importance of building a professional online presence in today's digital era. The session emphasized that platforms such as LinkedIn are powerful tools for networking, career growth, and job opportunities. Students learned that meaningful professional connections, a strong personal brand, and an active digital presence can significantly enhance their employability. The session encouraged students to use their digital accounts strategically to showcase their skills, connect with the right people, and create better placement and career opportunities while reducing the challenges associated with traditional job search processes.
