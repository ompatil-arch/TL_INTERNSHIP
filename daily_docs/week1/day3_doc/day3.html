Autodesk Fusion — Step-by-Step Sign Up and Download Guide

Step 1- search Autodesk fusion on Google chrome or Microsoft edge

Step 2— Open Autodesk Website

Step 3 — Click “Get Fusion” or “Free Trial”

On the homepage:

• Find the button named:

o Free Trial

o Get Fusion

o Personal Use

Click on it.

Step 4— Choose Your Version

You will see different options:

Personal Use (Free)

Best for:

• Students

• Beginners

• Hobby users

Education Version

For school and college students.

Paid Version

For professional and company use.

Choose the student version.

Step 5 — Create Autodesk Account

If you do not already have an account, open:

Fill in:

1. First Name

2. Last Name

3. Email Address

4. Password

5. Country

Then click:

“Create Account”

Step 6 — Verify Your Email

• Open your email inbox

• Find the Autodesk verification email

• Click the verification link

Your account is now ready.

Step 7 — Sign In to Autodesk

Return to the Fusion download page and sign in using:

• Your email address

• Your password

Step 8 — Start Download

Open the download page:

• Click Download Now

• Wait for the installer file to download

Step 9 — Open the Installer

After downloading:

• Go to your Downloads folder

• Double-click the Fusion installer file

Step 10 — Install Autodesk Fusion

Follow the installation process:

1. Click Install

2. Accept terms and conditions

3. Wait while files are installed

4. Allow internet access if asked

Installation may take several minutes.

Step 11— Launch Autodesk Fusion

After installation:

• Open Autodesk Fusion

• Sign in again with your Autodesk account

Now the software is ready to use
